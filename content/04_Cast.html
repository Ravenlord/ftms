Wolfgang Zarnack

Wolfgang is an actor from Mainz, Germany. He plays the 1st Lt. Alexander Baur, a young and ambitious officer of the German Air Force (aka "Luftwaffe"). As his role Wolfgang is very ambitious and he passed a very intensive job training over several years, including the well-known Mainz actor school and actor workshops at the DFFB (German film and television academy Berlin). Wolfgang is a well-known face from the German television and commercial platform. He is also known as actor playing at the Wiesbaden state theatre. His multiple actor awards confirms his intensive training over the last years.
For further information you can have a closer look at him on his website www.wolfgangzarnack.de.

Christian Weisz

There is no way to describe Christian's life in just one paragraph. Over the years Christian was not only an actor, but also stuntman, bodyguard, professional diver, racecar driver and martial arts sportsman. As multifaceted his life is, as multifaceted his role portfolio is. Christian describes himself as "the unique actor with the special skills". And indeed he is. With his life experience he was the first choice for playing the role of Marcel Pion, the Warrant Officer class I in the co-pilot seat, next to the main actor Wolfgang. He plays the experienced and hardened counterpart to 1st Lt. Alexander Baur.
To have a closer look into Christian's portfolio visit his website via www.christianweisz.com.
